# 🥚 EggTracker Management System

EggTracker Management System is a web-based egg production, inventory, and distribution management platform designed for a poultry farm business in the Philippines.

The system helps the poultry farm efficiently manage egg production, inventory monitoring, customer orders, and delivery operations through digital tools and organized management features.

Currently, the system is focused on serving one poultry farm located within Iloilo Province.

---

# 📌 Features

## 📦 Egg Inventory Management
- Track available egg stocks in real time
- Record incoming and outgoing inventory
- Monitor egg categories and sizes
- Reduce manual inventory errors

## 🐔 Daily Egg Production Tracking
- Record daily egg production
- Monitor farm productivity
- Track damaged or rejected eggs
- Generate production history reports

## 🚚 Delivery & Distribution Tracking
- Manage customer orders
- Schedule deliveries
- Track outgoing deliveries
- Monitor delivery status and history

## 📊 Reports & Analytics
- Daily sales reports
- Inventory reports
- Production summaries
- Delivery reports
- Stock movement history

## 💬 Order Management
Users can:
- Manage customer orders
- Confirm delivery schedules
- Monitor transactions
- Organize customer records

## 📅 Delivery Scheduling
Authorized users can schedule and manage egg deliveries online.
